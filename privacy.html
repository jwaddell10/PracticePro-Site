PracticePro Volleyball – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 9/11/2025

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and password. This information is securely stored in our backend database powered by Supabase.

User-Generated Content: Premium users may upload photos of their own drills. These images, along with any drills or practice plans you create, are stored securely in our database to support app functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide access to app features like drill discovery and practice scheduling
  • Enable premium features such as drill uploads and backups
  • Maintain your account and personalize your experience

3. Subscriptions and Billing

We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions and billing. RevenueCat may process purchase receipts and subscription status, but we do not share your personal data with them beyond what is necessary for billing.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties. All data remains within our secure systems and is used solely to operate and improve the app.

5. User Control and Data Deletion

You may delete your account and associated data at any time through the app. If you need assistance, contact us at practiceprovolleyball@gmail.com.

6. Children’s Privacy

PracticePro Volleyball is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. Our VolleyCoach feature may be used by youth athletes; however, any account, upload, or interaction involving a minor must be initiated and supervised by a parent or guardian, who is responsible for reviewing and approving any video or content submitted on the minor's behalf. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided personal information to us without appropriate parental involvement, we will take steps to delete that information. Parents or guardians with questions or concerns about a minor's use of the app may contact us at practiceprovolleyball@gmail.com.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data, please reach out to us at: 📧 practiceprovolleyball@gmail.com

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and on our website.

9. VolleyCoach – Video Review Privacy Addendum

The VolleyCoach feature allows users to upload volleyball videos for private coaching review. Because this feature involves user-generated video content, the following additional privacy practices apply specifically to VolleyCoach.

Video Uploads

When you submit a video through VolleyCoach, we collect:

  • The video file you upload
  • Any notes or context you choose to include
  • Feedback provided by authorized coaches

All videos are stored securely in private Supabase Storage buckets. These videos are never publicly accessible, and access links expire automatically.

How Your Videos Are Used

Uploaded videos are used only for:

  • Providing coaching feedback
  • Supporting your personal training experience within the VolleyCoach feature

Videos are not shared publicly, not used for marketing, and not distributed to third parties.

Access to Your Videos

Your uploaded videos are accessible only to:

  • You
  • Authorized VolleyCoach reviewers
  • Internal systems required to deliver coaching feedback

No other users can view your videos.

Video Retention

To protect user privacy, uploaded videos are not stored indefinitely. Videos are automatically deleted after:

  • 30 days, or
  • After coaching feedback has been delivered, depending on your selected preference

You may request early deletion at any time by contacting us.

User Responsibility

By uploading a video, you confirm that:

  • You have permission to upload the content
  • The video does not contain inappropriate or harmful material
  • The video is intended solely for coaching purposes

Youth Athlete Safety

VolleyCoach may be used by youth athletes. Parents or guardians should ensure they approve any video uploads made by minors. All videos remain private and are reviewed only for coaching purposes.
